About Westward – What Even Is This Game?

Howdy, partner. Welcome to Westward, a multiplayer open-world game set during the wild and gritty days of U.S. western expansion (1803–1861). But this ain’t just another cowboy game. Here’s the deal:

What’s the game actually about?

  • Build stuff – Form towns, build up communities, or start your own tribe. Play solo or with friends.
  • Explore huge maps – Inspired by Yellowstone, southern Utah, and other gorgeous spots from the American West.
  • Choose your side – Settlers seeking a new life, or tribal leaders protecting their land.
  • Make your own story – Be peaceful, aggressive, sneaky, creative… we won’t stop you.
  • Mess around with friends – Or strangers. Or frenemies. Just don’t get shot. Or do. It’s your journey.

Wait… what’s the Cyfrex thing?

  • The Cyfrex are a DLC faction — a cold-adapted lizard race with feathers, honor, and great hair (if feathers count).
  • They use hit-and-run tactics and have deep spiritual beliefs tied to the land.
  • You won’t meet them right away… but when you do, you’ll remember.

So what’s this server for?

  • We’re building the game right now and need creative volunteers (writers, artists, devs, lore nerds, anyone cool).
  • A place where fans and curious folks can chill, chat, and keep up with progress.
  • Basically: if you think building something awesome from scratch with a cool community sounds fun, you’re in the right place.

Important notes:

  • There are no dumb questions.
  • Feedback is gold.
  • You don’t need to be a pro — just be excited.

Saddle up. Let’s build the frontier!
